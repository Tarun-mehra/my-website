## Page Sections

### Index.html (Main Page)
- **Navigation Bar**: Logo + Article Links
- **Hero Section**: Blog title with animated background
- **Article Grid**: 6 article cards with preview images
- **About Section**: Brief blog description
- **Footer**: Copyright and social links

### Individual Article Pages
- **Navigation Bar**: Consistent across all pages
- **Article Header**: Title, date, reading time
- **Hero Image**: AI-generated relevant imagery
- **Article Content**: Formatted blog post with typography hierarchy
- **Related Articles**: Links to other posts
- **Footer**: Consistent footer
